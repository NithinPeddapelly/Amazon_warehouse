1. Product Vision
Build a personal job-monitoring platform that continuously monitors Amazon Jobs and future job
sources, detects new jobs before public Telegram repost channels, filters only relevant Scotland
jobs, and immediately sends alerts to Telegram.
Telegram acts only as a delivery mechanism.
The scraper and monitoring engine perform the actual work.

2. Problem Statement
Current methods create delays:
в—ЏвЂ‹ Telegram channels repost jobs after detection delay
в—ЏвЂ‹ Users manually refresh websites
в—ЏвЂ‹ Warehouse jobs disappear quickly
в—ЏвЂ‹ Users receive irrelevant jobs from all over the UK
в—ЏвЂ‹ Fast-moving listings often fill before users discover them
Goal:
Enable the user to receive Scotland job alerts before public channels.
